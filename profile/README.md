# Know Nepal 🇳🇵

Know Nepal is an open-source organization dedicated to building reliable digital resources about Nepal.

Our goal is to develop high-quality software, APIs, and open datasets that make information about Nepal accessible, well-structured, and reusable. Through modern technologies and open collaboration, we aim to support developers, researchers, students, travelers, organizations, and the wider community.

---

## Mission

We believe access to accurate and well-organized information is essential for education, innovation, and informed decision-making. Know Nepal is committed to building open-source solutions that improve the accessibility, quality, and usability of Nepal-related information while fostering community collaboration.

---

## What We Build

Our projects span multiple domains, including:

- **Geography** — Administrative divisions, geographic data, and GIS resources.
- **Public Services** — Healthcare, education, and other public information.
- **Tourism & Culture** — Destinations, heritage, history, traditions, and arts.
- **Developer Infrastructure** — Public APIs, open datasets, SDKs, and developer tools.

As the organization grows, we will continue expanding our ecosystem with new projects and initiatives.

## Principles

Our work is guided by the following principles:

- **Open Source** — Software, documentation, and datasets are developed openly whenever possible.
- **Reliable Information** — We strive to provide accurate, well-maintained, and trustworthy data.
- **Community Collaboration** — We welcome contributors from all backgrounds and experience levels.
- **Quality & Simplicity** — We value clean software, clear documentation, and maintainable systems.
- **Sustainable Development** — We build projects designed to grow and remain useful over time.

## Contributing

Know Nepal is a community-driven initiative, and contributions of all sizes are welcome.

Whether you're fixing bugs, improving documentation, developing new features, or contributing datasets, your work helps strengthen Nepal's open-source ecosystem.

To get started:

1. Explore our repositories.
2. Look for issues labeled `good first issue`.
3. Fork a repository and create a feature branch.
4. Submit a pull request.

Our vision is to build a trusted open-source ecosystem that provides high-quality software, APIs, and digital resources about Nepal for developers, researchers, institutions, and the public.
